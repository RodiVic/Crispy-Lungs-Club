Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 What if I mess up?

That's totally normal! Quitting isn't linear. Your squirrel buddy understands that setbacks happen. The important thing is getting back on track. Every attempt teaches you something new about your triggers and what works for you.

⏰ Do I have to quit forever?

Not necessarily! Some people aim for complete cessation, others focus on harm reduction. The goal is whatever feels right for you. Even cutting back significantly can have health benefits. Your squirrel is here to support whatever journey you're on.

⏳ How long do cravings last?

Most intense cravings peak in the first 3-5 minutes and usually pass within 10-20 minutes. The first few days are typically the hardest, but it gets easier! Physical withdrawal symptoms usually subside within a few weeks.

🐌 Cold turkey or slow down?

Both approaches work for different people! Cold turkey can be faster but more intense. Gradual reduction can be easier to manage but takes longer. Listen to your body and choose what feels sustainable for you. You can always adjust your approach.
